Yes! Join Us for a Night at the Museum: Exploration After Dark!

Embark on an unforgettable adventure as we invite you to spend a night at our museum. Explore the wonders of science under the cover of darkness, where the exhibits come alive and the museum transforms into an extraordinary playground for exploration and discovery. Bring your sleeping bag and join us!

Sleepover with the Dinosaurs:

Experience the thrill of sleeping under the watchful eyes of our ancient dinosaur inhabitants. Bring your sleeping bags and settle in for a night surrounded by prehistoric wonders.

Nighttime Expeditions:

Embark on guided nighttime expeditions through the museum galleries, led by expert educators. Discover hidden secrets, encounter nocturnal creatures, and delve into the mysteries of the natural world after hours.

Evening Entertainment:

Unwind with evening entertainment, including storytelling sessions, science-themed movies, and campfire songs. Gather around for a night of laughter, learning, and camaraderie.

Midnight Snack:

Recharge with a midnight snack provided by our museum staff. Enjoy tasty treats and refreshments to keep you energized throughout the night.

Important Information:

  • Age Requirement: This event is suitable for families with children aged 5 and above. All children must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.
  • Registration: Space is limited, so be sure to register early to secure your spot. Visit our website or contact us directly to reserve your tickets.
  • What to Bring: Don't forget to bring your sleeping bag, pillow, pajamas, toiletries, and any personal items you may need for an overnight stay.
  • Safety Measures: Your safety is our top priority. We will adhere to all safety guidelines and protocols to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all participants.
